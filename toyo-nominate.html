WHAT IS TOYO!

TOYO!, or Ten Outstanding Young Omahans, is the oldest young professional award in the Omaha community. It was created by the Omaha Jaycees to recognize individuals between the ages of 21 and 40 who show exemplary commitment to improving the community through selfless acts of kindness while excelling in their professional careers.

HOW DOES TOYO! WORK

TOYO! is hosted by the Omaha Jaycees. Nominees for the 2018 TOYO! class are submitted by members of the Omaha community through the month of August. Application criteria and a panel of judges will determine the recipients, to be announced in October. Ten winners will receive a plaque and be recognized at the annual banquet to be held in February, 2018

HOW CAN I GET INVOLVED

Do you know someone that deserves recognition and celebration? TOYO! is looking for young professionals ages 21-40 who have made a long-lasting impact in Omaha, service in the community, and excelled in their professional careers. Self-nominate or nominate an individual today! Nominations close Friday, August 31, 2018 at midnight.
